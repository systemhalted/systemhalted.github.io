At 8:50am in the morning, my cell phone rang. I was sleeping at that time, so it took some time for me before I realised that my mobile was ringing. I picked it up to know that Gajendra was online. It was least expected as yesterday only he told me that he is going to Ratnagiri with some friends.

He told me the programme has been cancelled and asked me to reach office. I finished the morning chores in half an hour and left for office. It took 25 min to reach Sanghavi Phata (walking). I waited for Infosys' bus and finally reached office by 6-seater. Gajendra and Pallav followed.

We decided to study for certification, but none of us could hardly read. We turned on music and went into our dreams.
