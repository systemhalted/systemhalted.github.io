There are several books and journals that are published by[Publication Division](http://publicationsdivision.nic.in/), [Ministry of Information and Broadcasting](http://mib.nic.in/), [GOI](http://goidirectory.nic.in/). One of the journal is [Yojana](http://www.yojana.gov.in/). Yojana [is](http://www.yojana.gov.in/about.asp) a monthly devoted to socio-economic issues and started its publication in 1957 with Mr. Khuswant Singh as its Chief Editor. The magazine is now published in 13 languages viz. English, [Hindi](http://yojana.gov.in/hindi/default.asp), [Urdu](http://yojana.gov.in/hindi/regional/urdu.htm), [Punjabi](http://yojana.gov.in/hindi/regional/punjabi.htm), [Marathi](http://yojana.gov.in/hindi/regional/marathi.htm), [Gujarati](http://www.yojana.gov.in/regional/gujarati.htm), [Bengali](http://www.yojana.gov.in/regional/bengali.htm), [Assamese](http://www.yojana.gov.in/regional/assamese.htm), [Telugu](http://www.yojana.gov.in/regional/telugu.htm), [Tamil](http://www.yojana.gov.in/regional/tamil.htm), [Kannada](http://www.yojana.gov.in/regional/kannada.htm), [Malayalam](http://www.yojana.gov.in/regional/malayalam.htm)and [Oriya](http://www.yojana.gov.in/regional/oriya.htm).
