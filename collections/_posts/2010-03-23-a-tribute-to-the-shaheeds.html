Today is 23rd March 2010. 69 years ago, on 23rd March 1931 - Bhagat Singh, Sukhdev and Rajguru became martyrs for this land of ours. Wish we have more people like them who devote their lives for the country. My tributes to the trio!!

SHAHEEDON KI CHITAON PAR LAGENGE HAR BARAS MELE, WATAN PE MITNE WALON KA YAHI BAKI NISHAN HOGA!!
