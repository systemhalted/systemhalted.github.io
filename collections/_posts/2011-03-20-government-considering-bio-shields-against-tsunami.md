I was going through this news sometime back  so thought it is worth sharing. Here are the excerpts:

- The Environment Ministry is considering the idea of developing bioshields comprising mangrove and non-mangrove species in coastal areas adjoining critical infrastructure projects such as [power plants](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Power_station "Power station") and oil storage depots.

- The idea of promoting [mangroves](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Mangrove "Mangrove") and other biological shields to provide a ‘speed breaker’ was suggested by [agricultural scientist](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Agricultural_science "Agricultural science") and [Rajya Sabha](http://rajyasabha.nic.in "Rajya Sabha") (Council of States) MP [MS Swaminathan](http://www.msswaminathan.com/). The suggestion is being followed up by the expert group appointed by Environment Minister [Jairam Ramesh](http://www.jairamramesh.in/profile/profile.html# "Jairam Ramesh") to evaluate additional safeguards against the risk of tsunami.

- Swaminathan has also suggested that the coastal areas adjoining the [nuclear plants](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nuclear_power "Nuclear power") could be declared as critically vulnerable coastline. The senior agricultural scientist said that the idea of using mangroves as line of defence against coastal storms and [tsunamis](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Tsunami "Tsunami") came following a discussion with older generation of Japanese scientists in 1989.
