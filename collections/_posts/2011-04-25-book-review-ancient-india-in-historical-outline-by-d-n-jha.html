[caption][/captionIt is a wonderful book for those who want to read and understand Ancient India. A nice book for starters as it gives you a feel of Ancient Indian History. Ancient History being weird in the sense that there are no personalities involved and the only source that remains is archaeological evidences, it is difficult to comprehend these things. There is a logic involved and to understand that logic the presentation must be great and unique. This is the case with Ancient India by D N Jha.

I rate it 4/5.
