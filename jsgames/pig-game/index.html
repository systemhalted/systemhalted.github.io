How to Play?

  1. Pig is two player dice game.
  2. Player 1 starts the game by rolling the dice.
  3. Player rolling the dice accumulate points by pressing Hold.
  4. When the player rolls a One, players switch.
  5. GOAL: Whoever accumulates 100 points first, wins.
